About Me.

Growing up in a small rural town in New Jersey, I learned a lot about farming and taking care of animals. My parents and all three of my siblings have careers in law, however, I decided to pursue a different path. Due to my natural inclination toward mathematics and the like, I decided to study computer science and mathematics. Many of my skills are self-taught, however the professors and resources available to me at Rutgers University have been highly beneficial in my progression as a software developer. Every project assigned to me over these past four years has been challenging yet highly enjoyable, so I am sure I made the right choice in selecting my career.

While I spend most of my time doing schoolwork and programming new projects, I also have other interests and hobbies I enjoy. I love sports- whether I am watching or playing. I am in a fantasy football league with my friends from my hometown, and I also play basketball with them when I have time on weekends. I love to play video games, read, and bake, and I am a big film buff. Programming has brought me so many hours of frustration and so many hours of joy. There is no better feeling than completing a project that I spent days researching and programming for. I am looking forward to graduating from Rutgers in May 2020, and I am currently searching for a job in software development.
