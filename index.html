About Me Thomas Hanna

Growing up in Farmingdale New Jersey I learned a lot about farming and taking care of animals. My parents, my brothers, and my sister all studied law. Unlike everyone I knew I decided to study computer science and mathematics. Throughout my life I self-taught most of my skills and I continue to learn more about computer science every day. Every project and school assignment has been challenging and fun so I know I picked the right career choice. Programming new projects and doing school work is how I spend most of my time but I also have some hobbies too. I love to watch football, I am in a fantasy football league with my hometown friends, I enjoy playing basketball, I watch tons of movies and shows, I cook and bake almost every day, I'm a big Star Trek: NG fan, and I play video games. Programming has brought me so many hours of frustration and so many hours of joy, I'm currently looking for a career in software development.

Crypto-Chat

CryptoChat is designed to be a discrete messaging service that uses asymmetric cryptography to securely send messages to your friends or business partners. Written in Java. Privacy on the internet is very important and CryptoChat is a great way to make sure your messages aren’t read by third parties.
